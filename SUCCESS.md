### Cách 2: Manual Deploy
1. Truy cập: https://vercel.com/dashboard
2. Click **"New Project"**
3. Chọn **"Import Git Repository"**
4. Paste URL: `https://github.com/ducminh0302/real_estate`
5. Click **"Deploy"**
